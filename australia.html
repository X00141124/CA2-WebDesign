Australia

Holiday in Australia

Take in the iconic sights, historic architecture, lively shopping and vibrant nightlife beating in Sydney's heart. Then soak up the sunshine and embrance the laid back lifestyle on one of the nearby beaches.

The perfect holidays in Sydney intersperse fantastic attractions with an enviable list of outdoor pursuits. Head to the harbour area and explore the wold famous Sydney Opera House or try the bridge climb for truly spectacular city views.

New Zealand

Holiday in New Zealand

New Zealand is all about nature in all her pomp and majesty with some truly eye-popping natural attractions.

Feel the heat in North Island with hissing geysers, hot springs and volcanoes like White Island and Mount Tarawera. In the south, glacial ice has scoured dramatic valleys from the peaks of majestic snow capped Southern Alps all the way to the awesome inspiring sounds of Fiordland National Park.

Fiji

Holiday in Fiji

An exotic melting pot of cultures and traditions, home to pristion beaches and lush, tropical hinterland. Resorts and hotels in Fiji vary from family friendly accommodations to luxurious and exclusive resorts and island hideaways for adults, most with fabulous spas and some with beautiful golf courses.

The island of Fiji enjoy a typical climate throughout the year with high temperatures and humidity the norm. Fiji has on average around six to eight hours of sunshine a day with fairly consistent temperatures throughout the year.
